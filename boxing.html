The passion for boxing

I've always wanted to learn a combat sport, as a motivation to keep fit and my mind sharp. That's when I decided to get myself a pair of gloves and learn boxing

As suprisingly as it may be to some people, boxing requires alot of thinking and almost every move is calculated. Stepping into the ring with someone is the same as playing a game of chess with him- you have to keep anticapating his every move in order to stay ahead.

Boxing has enabled me to make friends who push me to overcome physical and mental barriers that I place on myself. As the captain of NUS boxing, I am proud to lead a group of humble and earnest individuals in bringing the sport to a new level.
